# Accessibility (a11y) Standards

This document collects accessibility standards, best practices, naming conventions and guidance for building inclusive UI.

A screen reader helps users with limited or no vision access and interact with digital content via audio, touch, or keyboard. Main screen-reader users are people who are blind or have very low vision.
